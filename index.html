What Tangelo Does

Tangelo is a Venture Studio that accelerates business growth for mid-sized companies and an Innovation Lab that helps mid-sized companies digitally transform. Through our highly efficient, one-stop-shop and vertically integrated business model, we work with successful mid-sized companies who have the potential to grow digitally. Our specialized teams, a mix of highly experienced talent in business strategy, product design, digital marketing and deep technology are your strongest assets in accelerating digital transformations. Whether part of our Venture Studio or our Innovation Lab, we create opportunities aimed at increasing your company's valuation through our commitment to Build What's Next. Together. Our approach to planning, implementing and growing novel, disruptive and agile digital transformations.
