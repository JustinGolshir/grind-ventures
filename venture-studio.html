Venture Program Overview

We work with a diverse range of venture models that benefit both the portfolio company and Tangelo.

After an initial discovery phase, see an example of our venture model shown below. Using a fixed date duration, we consider:

  • a fee model,
  • a revenue share model (5%-25%) that is either fixed or incremental or
  • a combination of both models.

All scenarios can potentially include a percentage share of equity ownership.

The Venture Model

With our core values and expertise, we define venture partnership through two forms of investment: sweat equity and capital. Unlike other firms in the market, we work with all stages/ phases of a company on all types of problem solving. We provide all the ingredients necessary for mid-sized companies to accelerate their business growth. Our start-to-end general roadmap includes the following steps:

We provide all the ingredients necessary for emerging companies to form and flourish into successful businesses.

Selective process accepts exceptional startups & early stage or emerging small-mid sized companies.
Variables of the market, maturity, etc. are weighed to determine the best fit fee and/or revenue share model. Each partnership agreement and path is unique.
Partnership is formed.
Percentage share of equity ownership can be identified throughout the partnership process.
Discovery Phase: A fixed duration where an evaluation is conducted. Tangelo actively provides exemplary talent and strategy to enable company scaling and growth.
Re-evaluation post discovery research to determine adjustments to the venture model. Consider reducing or waiving fees for future equity.
Business continues to scale with Tangelo, exits or engages in an IPO.
